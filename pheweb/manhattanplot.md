# GWAS overview

Clicking on any phenotype will show you an overview of the [GWAS](../methods/phewas/) results:

* Detailed info about **phenotype definition**
* **Manhattan plot**
* List of **top hits**
* **Q-Q-plot**

## Manhattan plot

Clicking on any point will lead you to the[ locus zoom view](locus-zoom.md).
