# PheWAS

### Principles

* All positions are on GRCh38
* Variant identifiers are \`chr&lt;CHR&gt;\_&lt;POS&gt;\_&lt;REF&gt;\_&lt;ALT&gt;\`
* Chromosomes are either indicated with numbers \(\`1-25\`\) or letters \(\`chr1-chr22\`, \`chrX\`, \`chrY\`, \`chrMT\`\)
